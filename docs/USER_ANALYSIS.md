- **Single-domain only** — only knows Groww portfolio, no spending/health/career

- **Validates our thesis but doesn't threaten cross-domain moat**

## 5. PRIVACY & TRUST — Will They Share Data?

### SMS Access

| Status | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Precedent | Walnut (15M+ users), MoneyView, Fi — all read bank SMS |
| Grant rate | ~46% of users grant SMS/data access to payment apps |
| **CRITICAL BLOCKER** | **Google Play banned READ_SMS** for non-default SMS apps effective Jan 28, 2026 |
| Workaround | SMS Retriever API (hash-based, no permission) or email-only tracking |

**This is a BLOCKER for OpenClutch's current architecture.** The READ_SMS permission will be rejected by Play Store. Must pivot to SMS Retriever API or rely on email-based transaction detection.

### Gmail Access

| Status | Detail |

| Precedent | INDmoney offers Gmail integration for auto-tracking |
| Trust barrier | 82% say data protection is crucial for trust |

## 7. MARKETING — How to Acquire Users

### The One Hook Feature: "Hafta Roast" (Weekly Spending Roast in Hinglish)

**Why this wins:**
- Cleo proved spending roasts = growth engine ($280M ARR, doubled revenue 4 years straight)
- No Indian app does Hinglish spending roasts
- Hinglish content gets **20-30% higher engagement** than English on Instagram
- Zero onboarding needed — just SMS/email permission → instant roast
- Screenshot-worthy → WhatsApp-forwardable → organic virality

**Example roast:**
> *"Bhai, tune Zomato pe Rs 4,200 uda diye is hafte. Tera monthly salary se zyada Zomato pe jaa raha hai. Kya tu Zomato ka shareholder hai? Nahi? Toh band kar."*

**Secondary hook:** "Kya Main Afford Kar Sakta Hoon?" (Can I Afford This?)
- Input: photo of something you want to buy
- Output: AI verdict based on your real salary, EMIs, spending patterns
- Shareable: "My AI said I can't afford this iPhone" = viral content
