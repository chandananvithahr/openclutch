## Direct Competitors

### 1. Cleo — Closest Global Competitor
- **What:** AI money assistant — connects bank + cards, budgets, cash advances
- **Funding:** $175M | Valuation: $500M
- **Users:** 7M+ (US + UK)
- **Pricing:** Free basic | $2.99–$19.99/month premium
- **Tone:** Playful, "roast mode", Gen Z focused
- **Strong at:** Conversational finance AI, subscription detection, personality
- **Missing:**
  - Finance ONLY — no trading, health, career, family
  - US/UK only — zero India presence
  - No Indian broker integrations
  - No RAG memory
- **Threat level:** LOW — different market, single domain

---

### 2. INDmoney — Biggest India Competitor
- **What:** Wealth management super app — stocks, mutual funds, US stocks, IPOs
- **Funding:** $158M | Tiger Global, Steadview Capital
- **Users:** 10M+
- **Pricing:** Freemium
- **Strong at:** Multi-asset portfolio tracking, Indian + US markets, 10M users
- **Missing:**
  - No conversational AI — you can't ask it questions
  - Finance/investment only — no health, career, family
  - No proactive intelligence
  - No Gmail, no health data
- **Threat level:** MEDIUM — large user base but not AI-first

---

### 3. Fi Money — No Longer a Threat
- **What:** Indian neobank with AI insights
- **Funding:** $169M | Temasek, Ribbit Capital
- **Status:** ⚠️ WINDING DOWN consumer banking as of March 2026
- **Why they failed:** Couldn't compete in Indian banking. Pivoting to B2B AI.
- **Lesson for Clutch:** Consumer banking is hard. Don't become a bank. Be an AI layer on top.
- **Threat level:** NONE — exiting consumer market

---

### 4. Monarch Money — US Personal Finance AI
- **What:** Connects 10,000+ US financial institutions, AI assistant, budgeting
- **Funding:** $75M Series B (May 2025) | Valuation: $850M
- **Users:** Explosive growth after Mint shutdown (20x growth)
- **Pricing:** ~$99/year subscription
- **Strong at:** Net worth tracking, AI weekly recaps, replacing Mint
- **Missing:**
  - US ONLY — no India
  - Finance only — no health, career, family
  - No Indian broker APIs
- **Threat level:** LOW — US only, different market

---

### 5. Copilot — US Finance App
- **What:** AI personal finance, auto-tags transactions, adaptive budgets
- **Funding:** Private
- **Pricing:** $95/year
- **Strong at:** Privacy-first, no ads, clean UI, learns patterns
- **Missing:** US only, finance only, no India
- **Threat level:** LOW — US only

---

### 6. Dot by New Computer — Dead
- **What:** Personal AI companion that remembers your life
- **Status:** ⚠️ SHUT DOWN October 2025
- **Why:** Couldn't monetize. Beautiful product, no business model.
- **Lesson for Clutch:** Memory alone is not a product. Must be tied to real data and real actions.
- **Threat level:** NONE — shut down

---

### 7. Rewind / Limitless — Acquired
- **What:** AI that records everything you see and hear
- **Status:** ⚠️ ACQUIRED by Meta (December 2025)
- **Lesson:** Personal AI is valuable enough for Meta to buy. Validation.
- **Threat level:** NONE for now — being absorbed into Meta

## The Big Tech Threat

| Company | What they could build | Timeline | How to survive |
|---------|----------------------|----------|----------------|
| Google | "Google Life Assistant" with all your data | 12–24 months | Move fast, build loyalty, own the Indian market first |
| Meta | Personal AI (post-Limitless acquisition) | 12–18 months | They're US-focused, India is your moat |
| Zerodha | Zerodha AI assistant | 6–12 months | Only shows Zerodha data. You show everything. |
| Groww | Groww GR1 (beta now) | 3–6 months | Only shows Groww data. You show everything. |

**The broker threat is real but limited.** Each broker will build AI for their own data. None will show competitor data. Clutch always wins on completeness.
